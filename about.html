--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About

I am a Research Fellow in the Developmental Biology Program at the Sloan Kettering Institute, New York. I am an evolutionary biologist by training and received my Ph.D. in evolutionary genetics from the University of Rochester, New York. My research is broadly in the areas of evolutionary and population genomics; small RNA-mediated immunity against selfish genetic elements; and the evolution of small RNA pathways in animals. I use fruit flies Drosophila as a model organism to study a variety of interesting biological questions.
