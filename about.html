--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About

I am a Research Fellow in the Developmental Biology Program at the Sloan Kettering Institute, New York I am an evolutionary biologist by training with interests in evolutionary and population genomics; molecular evolution; genomic conflict and selfish genetic elements; and the evolution of small RNA pathways in animals. I use fruit flies as a model organism to study a variety of interesting biological questions.
